== Installation ==

**From your WordPress dashboard**

* Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
* Search for 'Woocommerce Custom Print'
* Activate 'Woocommerce Custom Print' from your Plugins page.

**From WordPress.org**

* Download 'Woocommerce Custom Print'.
* Upload the 'Woocommerce Custom Print' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
* Activate 'Woocommerce Custom Print' from your Plugins page.

1. While adding the product, check this box, if you want this product to be custom printed.

3. After that you have chosen the image and submitted, you'll get a page to choose the design of image to be printed like half-drop, half-bricks, etc.

4. After successful order, you'll see the image url of the customer and custom design chosen by customer
